# PrimateScope AI — Product Brief

> *"Turn 500 hours of camera trap footage into publishable behavioral findings — in 15 minutes."*

---

## The Problem

Wildlife monitoring generates massive audiovisual datasets. A single camera trap study produces:

- **500+ hours** of footage per field season
- **3–6 months** of manual frame-by-frame review by trained personnel
- **$40,000–$80,000** in labor costs for a single publishable dataset
- **Critical behavioral events missed** due to human fatigue and review bottlenecks

In primatology specifically, the subtlety of social signals — brief grooming bouts, displacement gestures, low-intensity chases — routinely escape manual annotation.

---

## Our Solution

**PrimateScope AI** is an edge-deployable field intelligence platform that:

1. **Runs entirely offline** on local hardware — no cloud dependency
2. **Processes camera trap footage in real-time** using primate-specific YOLO detection
3. **Classifies 5 behavioral states** with per-frame confidence scores
4. **Identifies and tracks individuals** across sessions via unique markings
5. **Surfaces publication-ready research insights** automatically
